About Library

The Library and Information centre is an education and research resource center (ERRC) with multiple resources which cater to the needs of study and reference for the students, faculty and staff. The Print Journals, E-Journals, Project Reports, Video Lectures, C.Ds, Newspapers and Magazines etc., promote awareness and profundity among erudite. The library has more than 31304 titles which comprise academic, research, competitive and general books. Apart from subject related books, the library also has a good collection of general reading books in variegated fields like :

  • Classics
  • Fiction
  • Biographies & Autobiographies : Relating to great Scientists
  • Personality development (Careers, Placement & Group discussions)
  • Self help (puzzles, brainteasers)
  • Spiritual
  • Philosophical
  • English Language and Training (ELT)
  • Ethics & human values
  • And also books like Dictionaries, Encyclopedias and Almanacs & Handbooks.

Through INDEST-AICTE consortium membership, IEL online facility we are accessing nearly 276 online journals.

All students, faculty and staff members of the Institute are entitled to make use of the Library facilities. Initially setup in 2001, the Central Library moved to its new independent Palatial building in April, 2012.

 

Mission

Library serves as a resource center and aims to develop a comprehensive collection of document’s useful for faculty and students of the institute and provides an efficient dissemination of knowledge.

 

Vision

  • To provide quality service to the users
  • To disseminate technical knowledge
  • To offer modern tools for accessing information
  • To network with leading libraries

 

Short term goals

To serve the academic and research needs of all students and faculty members. As a center for collection of books and Journals of all related subjects to develop a comprehensive collection of information required for teaching, reference and research purpose. To acquire, organize Information resources to meet the present and future needs of its users.

 

Long term goal

  • To Develop a consortium of all Engineering college libraries with resource sharing and networking

Facilities
Digital library

Digital Library consists of audio & video course materials, Tutorials, Notes, Question Papers, E-books etc. Modules of different Universities & Organizations like NPTEL, MIT, Stanford and Berkeley etc.

Online access to the e-resource of the Digital Library portals of IEEE, ASME, ASCE, Springerlink. These e-resources are IP enabled, can be accessed anywhere in the campus.

 

Inter Library Loan Facility

The library has DELNET (Developing Library Network) facility i.e. Resource sharing Library Network of nearly 1855 member libraries in India and abroad. Any kind of resource can be borrowed from other libraries with this service.


Internet Facility

In the proximity of the library building the institution has set up browsing centre with requisite infrastructure.


Highlights Of Our Library
  • The college has a good central library building an area of 2070 Sqm.
  • Stack Area : 828 Sqm.
  • Reading Area : 776.25 Sqm.
  • Library is automated with bar coded mechanisms for issue and returns and having OPAC facility.
  • The college has subscribed for EKALAVYA CHANNEL down-link for accessing Video lectures of various IIITS.
  • To create awareness among users, every year library personnel takes up “initiation of the freshman” program by taking orientation classes for all the first year of Diploma, B.Tech, M.Tech, M.B.A. and M.C.A. students. In this program awareness and orientation is being provided to the students on various facilities, resources and services available in our library and motivate them to utilize the library resources to the fullest extent possible.
